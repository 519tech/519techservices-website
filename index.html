519 Tech Services Inc. is a professional electronics repair shop located at 25 Industrial Dr., Unit 1C, Elmira, Ontario, Canada (N3B 3K3). Founded in January 2017 by Peter Giesbrecht and operating from a storefront since October 2022, 519 Tech Services offers expert repair services for phones, tablets, laptops, MacBooks, game consoles, and DJI drones. The business holds a 5.0-star Google rating based on 396+ reviews and has completed over 8,000 repairs.
